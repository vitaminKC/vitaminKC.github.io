KC's Keyboards!

Hello! I'm Casey and I like making custom keyboards and computers! I've been building computers and keyboards since 2021 and continue to research and design builds as a hobby. I've always had a passion for tinkering with things from taking apart mechanical pencils to building my family's full tower desktop. I've had the honor to help design and build computers and keyboards for friends and family. This hobby has become a major part of my life and I hope to continue sharing my passion with other people!
